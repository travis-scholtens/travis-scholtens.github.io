TrueSkill for Divisions

Travis Scholtens

Abstract

We present an extension of the TrueSkill and TrueSkill through time algorithms in the case of competitive leagues where players are separated into divisions, and primarily compete within their own division in a given season. By inferring skill independently among the players of each division, and then estimating skill differences across divisions, we improve upon the accuracy of both TrueSkill and TrueSkill through time in predicting the outcomes of future competitions.
